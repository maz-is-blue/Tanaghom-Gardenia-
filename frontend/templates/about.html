About Us

عن الجمعية

A non-profit choral society of two ensembles, founded in Beirut in {{ site.founded_year }}. We sing in Arabic and the wider repertoire, share a single rehearsal hall, and open our doors twice a year to anyone with a voice and the patience to use it well.

Mission

We exist to keep the choral tradition alive in Lebanon — patient, generous, and unmistakably ours.

{{ site.brand_en }} was founded in {{ site.founded_year }} by a small circle of singers who believed that the human voice — disciplined, joyful, and trained — remains the most honest expression of community. Seven seasons later, two ensembles share our home in Achrafieh: one a mixed choir working in the great European choral tradition, the other a chamber group devoted to Arabic art-song and the sacred repertoire of the Levant.

We perform in concert halls, churches, and gardens. We commission new work from Arab composers, restore neglected repertoire from regional archives, and run a youth outreach programme with three Beirut schools.
