Awards & Recognition

Internationally recognizedتقدير دولي

Gardenia has won production grants from Al Mawred Cultural Resource, AFAC, and Ettijahat for Independent Culture. In 2019, the choir won Best Regional Choir and Best Women's Choir at the Dubai Choir Festival — recognition of both artistic excellence and the social mission behind every performance.
