About Us

عن الجمعية

A non-profit choral society of two ensembles, founded in Beirut in 2019. We sing in Arabic and the wider repertoire, share a single rehearsal hall, and open our doors twice a year to anyone with a voice and the patience to use it well.

Mission

We exist to keep the choral tradition alive in Lebanon — patient, generous, and unmistakably ours.

Tanaghom Gardenia was founded in 2019 by a small circle of singers who believed that the human voice — disciplined, joyful, and trained — remains the most honest expression of community. Seven seasons later, two ensembles share our home in Achrafieh: one a mixed choir working in the great European choral tradition, the other a chamber group devoted to Arabic art-song and the sacred repertoire of the Levant.

We perform in concert halls, churches, and gardens. We commission new work from Arab composers, restore neglected repertoire from regional archives, and run a youth outreach programme with three Beirut schools.

History

Seven seasonsسبعة مواسم

2019
A small circle beginsبداية صغيرة

Eighteen singers gather in a borrowed church basement in Achrafieh. Our first conductor, Maya Khoury, leads the first rehearsal — a Palestrina motet, sung badly and joyfully.

2020
First public concertالحفل الأوّل

"Songs for a Difficult Year" — performed outdoors, in a private garden, four months after the August explosion. Two hundred guests, one rehearsal-less programme, the beginnings of an audience.

2021
Tanaghom is bornولادة تناغم

A chamber group splits from the main ensemble to focus on Arabic art-song and sacred repertoire. Sixteen voices, the same hall, a separate mission.

2022
First commissionالتكليف الأول

We commission Layla Saade to write a setting of three Adonis poems for double choir. The piece tours four cities; the recording reaches forty thousand listeners.

2023
Schools programmeبرنامج المدارس

Our outreach programme begins with three Beirut schools and forty-two children. Today, six schools and over a hundred children sing with us each season.

2024
The new rehearsal hallقاعتنا الجديدة

After two years of fundraising, we move into a permanent rehearsal hall — sprung floors, real acoustics, a piano that holds its tune. The first home we have called our own.

2026
Season sevenالموسم السابع

Forty active singers, two ensembles, a five-concert main season and a new commission from Hany Maalouf premiering at St. Joseph Church this July.
