Two ensembles share our rehearsal hall — one in the great choral tradition, one devoted to Arabic art-song and sacred music. Different repertoires, the same room, twice a week.

Professional Women's Choir · سوبرانو · ميتزو · ألتو

Gardeniaغاردينيا

A professional all-female choir of soprano, mezzo-soprano, and alto voices, bringing together young women musicians and singers from various regions across Syria. Gardenia presents vocal works of the highest standard while building bridges among Syrians through the healing power of music.

Youth Choir · ٢٥٠+ صوت

Tanaghomتناغم

A Syrian family that includes young men and women from all walks of life, backgrounds, and regions across Syria. Founded in 2019 in Sahnaya, rural Damascus, in partnership with UNDP, Tanaghom uses music as a tool for positive communication, peacebuilding, and social cohesion.
